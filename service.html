Research and Discovery

The first step in the design process is to conduct research and gather information about the business or organization. This includes understanding the company's values, target audience, competition, and overall brand message.

Concept Development

Once the research is complete, the designer will begin brainstorming and sketching out the initial design concepts. These concepts will be presented to the client for feedback and input.

Design Refinement

Based on the client’s feedback, the designer will refine the chosen concept and create a more detailed and polished version of the design. This may include experimenting with different color schemes, typography, and other design elements.

Finalization

Once the design is finalized, the designer will create various file formats for different uses, such as print or digital applications. The final files will be delivered to the client, along with guidelines on how to properly use the design.

Delivery

The designer will deliver the final files to the client, along with guidelines on how to use the design correctly, such as recommended colors and sizes, and any restrictions on how the design can be modified.
